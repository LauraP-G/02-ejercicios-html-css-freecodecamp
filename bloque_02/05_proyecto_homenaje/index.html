"King of Pop"

Image from the music video 'Thriller'. The most listened to and well-known song by Michael Jackson. It was a great commercial success as well as critically acclaimed.

Here's a time line of Michael Jackson life:

  • 1958: Michael Jackson is born on August 29th in Gary, Indiana, USA.
  • 1964: The Jackson 5, with Michael as the lead vocalist, sign with Motown Records.
  • 1971: Michael Jackson begins his solo career with the album "Got to Be There".
  • 1982: Release of "Thriller", the best-selling album of all time.
  • 1983: Michael Jackson performs the iconic moonwalk for the first time on television during a performance on the "Motown 25: Yesterday, Today, Forever" special.
  • 1984: "Victory Tour" is launched, the final tour of the Jackson 5.
  • 1987: Release of the "Bad" album, producing a string of hits such as "Smooth Criminal" and "Man in the Mirror".
  • 1991: Release of the "Dangerous" album, featuring hits like "Black or White" and "Remember the Time".
  • 2009: Death of Michael Jackson on June 25th, at the age of 50.
  • 2010: Posthumous release of the album "Michael", featuring previously unreleased tracks by the late singer.
